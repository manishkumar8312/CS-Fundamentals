# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose 📘

This repository aims to provide a respectful and collaborative environment for learning and contributing to Computer Science fundamentals.

## 2. Expected Behavior 🤝

All contributors are expected to:

* Communicate respectfully and professionally
* Provide constructive feedback
* Focus on improving the quality of the content
* Be inclusive and considerate of others

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior 🚫

The following actions are not permitted:

* Harassment, discrimination, or offensive remarks
* Personal attacks or abusive language
* Irrelevant or spam contributions
* Plagiarism or failure to give proper credit

## 4. Contribution Standards 🛠

To maintain quality:

* Ensure contributions are relevant to CS fundamentals
* Keep explanations clear and well-structured
* Follow consistent formatting
* Provide proper attribution where necessary

## 5. Enforcement ⚖️

Maintainers may take appropriate action in response to violations, including:

* Removing or modifying contributions
* Restricting or banning contributors

## 6. Contact 📩

If you observe any violation of this Code of Conduct, please open an issue or contact the repository maintainer.

## 7. Acknowledgment ✔️

By participating in this repository, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
