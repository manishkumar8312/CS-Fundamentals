# Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Commitment

This repository is intended to provide a respectful and collaborative environment for learning and contributing to Computer Science fundamentals.

## 2. Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:

* Communicate respectfully and professionally
* Provide constructive and meaningful feedback
* Focus on improving the quality and clarity of the content
* Be inclusive and considerate of others

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following actions are not permitted:

* Harassment, discrimination, or offensive remarks
* Personal attacks or abusive language
* Irrelevant, low-quality, or spam contributions
* Plagiarism or failure to provide proper attribution

## 4. Contribution Standards

To maintain the quality of this repository:

* Ensure contributions are relevant to Computer Science fundamentals
* Keep explanations clear, concise, and well-structured
* Follow consistent formatting and organization
* Provide proper credit for referenced materials

## 5. Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to take appropriate action in response to violations of this Code of Conduct, including:

* Removing or modifying contributions
* Restricting or revoking contributor access

## 6. Contact

If you observe any violation of this Code of Conduct, please open an issue or contact the repository maintainer.

## 7. Acknowledgment

By participating in this repository, you agree to adhere to this Code of Conduct.
